About Our Company

Our Story

Kamier China Co.,Ltd was founded in 2005 with a simple mission: to manufacture the highest quality cutting tools for industrial applications. What began as a small workshop in Yangjiang, China has grown into a respected manufacturer serving clients worldwide.

Our Expertise

We specialize in scissors and cutters designed specifically for:

  • Steel and plastic strapping bands
  • Copper and aluminum wires
  • Electrical cables
  • Sheet metal and tin work

Our Facility

Our 800 square foot manufacturing facility houses state-of-the-art equipment operated by skilled technicians. We maintain strict quality control at every stage of production, from material selection to final inspection.

Our Values

Quality

We use only premium materials and rigorous testing to ensure our tools meet the highest standards.

Innovation

Continuous improvement drives our product development and manufacturing processes.

Integrity

We build lasting relationships through honesty, transparency, and reliability.
